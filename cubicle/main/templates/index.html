About Cubicle

What is it?

Cubicle is an online photo album production service that enables its users to participate in the photo album design process ranging from page design and layout to content determination and composition.

Target Audience

This service is particularly geared towards the production of photo albums for graduating students of an institution of learning like:

  • Higher Institutions (Universities, Polytechnics, FCEs)
  • Secondary Schools
  • Primary Schools
